## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå DON'T:
1. **Upload proprietary code** - Only documentation/guides
2. **Spam with links** - Keep it valuable and natural
3. **Ignore issues** - Respond within 48 hours
4. **Forget to update** - Commit at least monthly
5. **Be too promotional** - Focus on value, not selling
6. **Duplicate content** - Create unique GitHub-specific content
7. **Forget mobile** - Markdown renders on mobile

### ‚úÖ DO:
1. **Provide genuine value** - Real guides, not just ads
2. **Engage with community** - Reply to comments/issues
3. **Keep content updated** - Refresh annually
4. **Link naturally** - Context-relevant links
5. **Showcase expertise** - Demonstrate knowledge depth
6. **Be consistent** - Regular updates signal active project
7. **Cross-promote** - Link GitHub from your sites
